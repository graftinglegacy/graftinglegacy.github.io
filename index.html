Principals

Founded in 2017, Grafting Legacy LLC, is a private equity Search Fund sponsored by former executives of a public company currently valued at $18 billion to provide analytical and operational support in efforts to acquire businesses.

We focus towards opportunities to grow traditional small businesses in Atlanta by implement technology. We regularly review investment opportunities from thousands of banker intermediaries and direct outreach to thousands of business owners.

The following attributes possessed by Grafting Legacy’s Principals will allow them to be successful:

  • Strict compliance with non disclosure and non compete agreements
  • Strong support from network of advisors and experienced investors and operators
  • Certified ScrumMaster (CSM) - Agile Methodology Implementation
  • Executing operational improvements with senior leadership in order to increase EBITDA
  • Experience in corporate development, identifying market and business opportunities
  • Enterprise sales leadership experience

Nir Levy studied Venture Finance at Emory University’s Goizueta Business School (BBA). He is a serial entrepreneur with experience working for high growth technology companies in corporate development and vendor management. Nir is a founder of Campus Bubble, a technology company developing apps for Higher Education. He sold the platform to 12 universities before selling the business to Student Bridge. After that, Nir worked for Rainmaker Group, a technology company with revenue management products. Rainmaker Group subsequently sold its LRO product to RealPage for $300 million. Nir then worked for Rubicon Global, “Uber for trash”, where Nir’s team focused on a data and analytics approach to reducing supply chain costs while increasing service coverage to a national scope.

Ari Edlin studied Computer Information Systems within Georgia State University’s Robinson College of Business and is a Certified Scrum Master from his work for Zelis Healthcare. Ari’s unique professional experience is focused around managing enterprise software development projects through Agile in addition to implementing proprietary and vendor-owned technology. As a Senior Analyst, Ari reported directly to senior leadership and was responsible for defining software requirements. Ari was the key leader in launching a data cleansing product that resulted in $1.2 million in yearly recurring revenue. Ari’s family has several small business owners whom have lived in Atlanta for over six generations. He is uniquely positioned to relate to retiring business owners based in the Southeast.
